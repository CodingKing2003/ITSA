What is Escrow?

ESCROW was basically a virtual treasure hunt based on the skills of cryptography, cipher, coding and logical thinking. We had 3 rounds spread over 2 days. The first round was based on Mathematical and logical questions, second was based on technical and coding. Round 3 was based on cryptography and ciphers.
